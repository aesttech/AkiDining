About Us & Our Goals

We are Aki Dining. Our food is

We are a family owned business bringing high-quality food to Paulding County since 2015. Our goal is to bring the experience of culture-rich home-cooked meals to a restaurant like no other.

Diverse Options

Click to View Menu

Mexican Food

Derived from states all over Mexico, the variety of flavors are put into dishes ranging from tacos and tortas, to even combination plates which include multiple dishes.

Italian Food

Rooted from Italian recipes deep within the Aki family, these authentic dishes are sure to make your mouth water. From Ragu alla bolognese to creamy risotto, it will feel like you are in a home away from home.

Japanese Food

As a refined cuisine, Japanese cuisine is supported by a broad amount of seasonal and regional dishes. Soups, seafood, meats, and rice are all things that can pull together a perfect meal. Daily traditional specials!
